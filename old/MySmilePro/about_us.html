About Us

Get & Drop Logistics is one of the West Africa's leading community focused logistic and share ride app that provide hassle free transportation and delivery service. The company was founded March 2020 with Head Office in Lagos, Nigeria. Get & Drop is currently covering Lagos, Abuja, Ibadan, Abeokuta, Osogbo, Ile-Ife, Ilesha, Oyo and Ogbomosho, Kaduna, Kano, Onitsha and Porth court, working with partners and individual drivers to move people and packages from one point to another with VIP treatment. Our goal is to have continental coverage of the whole of Africa to provide reliable, hassle free transportation system using our world-class app that are cost effective and equitable for both riders and our partner drivers.
